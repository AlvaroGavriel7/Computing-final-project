Types of offense and consequences

Mild offense is a minor violation to the school regulation if a student did this the consequences will be:

  • Verbal warning
  • Red offense slip
  • Community service

Moderate offenses are more serious violations of school rules or repeated minor offenses that happen after previous warnings.

The consequences will be:

  • Parents will be called
  • Their scores might be affected in the report card
  • Written warning letter

Severe offense which are the most serious cases when a student keeps breaking the rules too often this will be directly held by the Student Affairs department starting from the first occurrence and so on
