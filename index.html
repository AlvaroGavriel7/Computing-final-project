Chapter 5 focuses on mentoring students by guiding them through the consequences of their actions. If its mild, moderate, or severe The goal is not only to correct mistakes but also to teach students how to become more responsible, respectful, and aware of their actions.

Punishment can make them reflect on what happened, take responsibility, and find ways to improve their behavior.
